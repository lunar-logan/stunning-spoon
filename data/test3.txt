Rice is the most important staple food. Rice is the seed of Oryza sativa or Oryza glaberrima. The Oryza includes 24 species.
Rice normally grows as an annual plant. Rice can also survive as a perennial and can produce a ratoon crop for upto 30 years.
Traditionally, rice is cultivated in flooded fields. Food from India primarily contains rice. Wheat is an important staple food after rice.
Food primarily contains wheat. Wheat is a grass belonging to genus triticum. Wheat has higher protein content then either maize or rice.
Floor is made from wheat. Floor is used extensively in bakery products which generally taste sweet.
